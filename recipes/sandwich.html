Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tsp jelly

Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of peanut butter on both slices of bread
  2. Apply a thin layer of jelly on one of the slices
  3. Place the slice with just peanut butter on top of the slice with jelly. Cut as desired
